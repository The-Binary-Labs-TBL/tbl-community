# Code of Conduct

We want `tbl-community` to be a place where contributors can ask questions, explore ideas, and disagree constructively without making the space hostile or exhausting.

## Expected behavior

- be respectful and welcoming
- give constructive, specific feedback
- assume good intent while still addressing concerns clearly
- help keep discussions focused and useful
- make space for contributors with different levels of experience

## Unacceptable behavior

- harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks
- repeated bad-faith arguing or provocation
- insulting, demeaning, or inflammatory language
- sharing private information without permission
- derailing discussions in ways that block productive collaboration

## Scope

This code of conduct applies to discussions, issues, pull requests, and other interactions in this repository.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, lock, hide, or remove content that violates these standards. They may also restrict participation when needed to protect the health of the community space.
